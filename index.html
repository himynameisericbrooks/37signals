Clarity is king

Buzzwords, lingo, and sensationalized sales-and-marketing-
speak have no place at 37signals. We communicate clearly

Our customers are our investors

Our customers fund our daily operations by paying for our
products. We answer to them - not investors, the stock

The basics are beautiful

We'll never overlook what really matters: The basics. Great
service, ease of use, honest pricing, and respect for our

We believe everyone is entitled to the best price we can offer.

same no matter who you are.

because you don't need it.
